Things to know:
"Saves" folder need to be in the same dictionary as Focus.jar in order to load save files

There is no delay for the AI, so it may appear they are being skipped. This is not the case,
they are just really fast thinkers

"Easy" AI chooses a random piece of their own, and moves it to a random legal location
"Hard" AI chooses their biggest stack, and moves it to a random legal location
